ABOUT US

Voiture Administration is an independent third-party administrator. Voiture administration was founded in 2016 by Asman Khan and Svenne van Voorthuijsen which is currently headquartered in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. The aim of starting the company was to provide insurance claims processing, billing, and remittance for auto repair and maintenance facilities around the country. We also offer a highly sophisticated proprietary claim system, comprehensive cost saving solutions and experienced employees.

MISSION

Our mission is to enable insurers to serve their policyholders more efficiently and effectively through collaborative relationships, process efficiency, advanced technology and data analysis.

VISION

Setting the standard for service excellence and client satisfaction for auto repair and maintenance facilities.
